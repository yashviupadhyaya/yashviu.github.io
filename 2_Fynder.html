DESIGN THINKING CASESTUDY - FYNDER

Building an environment renters and rentee using profile matching which would make the process for finding rooms and idle roommates faster.

OVERVIEW
This project was developed as a part of User experience Design Winter school in IIIT Hyderabad under the guidance of Prof. Aman Parnami and Prof. Ponurangam Kummargunu. We were teamed up into groups of four and asked to research about daily problems, conduct user research on the chosen problem and devise a solution. Our team consisted of Aditya Sher (Second Year student at IIIT Nagpur), Kishan Rawal (Software Engineer), Madhu Rali and me.

BRAINSTORMING - SEARCHING FOR A PROBLEM
Since most of us have at least once experienced visiting and living in a new city, we knew that it becomes difficult to search for a perfect place to live in. Additionally, having classmates who studied in the city but had their home elsewhere became our target users. We started asking basic questions related to their experiences in living in a new city, methods and problems for searching for an accommodation, finding a roommate, etc. Their answers to these questions helped us define a problem to work on.

  • To build a new and optimized information architecture in order to reduce the time taken by the user to retrieve the needed information.
  • To remove unnecessary fields and interactions and add new features to enhance user satisfaction.
  • To make cleaner and simpler user interfaces.

PROBLEM BRIEF
Many people need to shift themselves to a new place due to multiple reasons that majorly include education and jobs. These people need to find rooms on rent. Even though students have an option of staying in hostels, they face difficulties in following the strict schedules of these places. Due to which they miss their lunch or dinner and have to arrange it by themselves. Due to this, they too prefer living as paying guests or rent a room. However, renting a room for one becomes unaffordable and hence it becomes necessary for searching for someone to share the room and its rent. Currently, there does not exist any platform which particularly targets this problem.

USER RESEARCH
With our research we wanted to study the difficulties faced by the people that visit a new place in order to pursue higher studies, job or any other reason. We also wanted to understand their choice of living, preferences and the methods that they currently follow in order to find a place of accommodation and room mates if any. User Interviews were conducted to understand their problems and expectations. Based on our research insights, we were able to create a User Persona. Our personas focus on the usersâ€™ personality and lifestyle, motivations and their needs and frustrations.

IDEATION
We had a lot of current references which could be referred for their features. We listed down all the currently used applications along with their features which are used for either of the two purposes:

1. Find Rooms - Airbnb, Trivago, etc
2. Finding a preferred person - Linkedin, Tinder, Symbi, Roomi, etc

However, it was observed there exist no platform which targets both - finding a preferred room and a preferred roommate.

With this we were able to come up with a bunch of features for the required platform. We finally decided to move forward with the idea of designing an app which helps build a sense of community between the room finders, room mate finders and the room lenders - Fynder.
