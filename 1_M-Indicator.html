M-INDICATOR REDESIGN UX CASESTUDY

BRIEF
M-Indicator is a mobile application that primarily provides information about the timings, routes and fares of Local Trains and other services in the city of Mumbai. The application has been downloaded over 1 crore users and used by daily commutes of the city. With the fast moving life of the city it becomes essential for the commutators to be on time. For that an efficient transportation system is necessary. Additionally, important notifications about the timings and other related information should be at their fingertips. This makes the people rely on such an application.

However, if the information they are looking for is not easily searchable from the application and requires unnecessary interactions, the process becomes time-taking and frustrating. Thus it becomes necessary to redesign such applications, removing redundancy in the user interactions and making the task of searching for specific information easier and faster.

GOALS

  • To build a new and optimized information architecture in order to reduce the time taken by the user to retrieve the needed information.
  • To remove unnecessary fields and interactions and add new features to enhance user satisfaction.
  • To make cleaner and simpler user interfaces.

FIDINGS

Target users

M-indicator is majorly used by-

  • Office going people and students.
  • Their age ranges from 20 - 50 years.
  • Most of them are smart phone holders and use it to refer to for information related to their daily commute.
Purpose of use

M-indicator is majorly used for-

  • Checking Local Trains and their timings.
  • Checks bus numbers and their routes.
  • Use the notifications incase of any news related to trains and busses.


Defining Problems

  • 95% of the users use the transportation feature and no other features. Therefore, this needs to be separated from other features and given more emphasis.
  • The redundancy of the information architecture needs to be removed in order to avoid confusion.
  • All users except that they get the required information in the least possible time.
